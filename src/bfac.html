Facilities of Raja Mahendra Pratap Hostel

Resident Rooms

Raja mahendra pratap hostel has rooms on twin-sharing / single occupancy (for double bed occupancy) basis with a total capacity of 130 seats for boys and 100 seats for girls. Every room contains adequate furniture exclusively for each student like bed. cupboard,chair and table.

Mess

the hostel has a spacious mess having space for boys and girls. nutritious food is served in neat and clean surroundings at subsidized rates. the mess has a saparate section for staff members, guests and residents.

Common Room

The hostel has common rooms (separately for boys and girls) where residents can read daily newspaper and magazines. Television with cable facilities provided in the common room.

Indoor games room

Table tennis, chess and carom board can be played by residents in their space time. however the games room shall remain cosed during exam time.
